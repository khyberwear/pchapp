  - "The Peshawari Chappal is more than just a shoe; it's a symbol of Pashtun culture and tradition. Originating from the city of Peshawar, this unique footwear has a history that dates back over a century."
  - "Traditionally worn with Shalwar Kameez, the chappal is known for its comfort and durability. The design features two wide straps that cross over the foot and a heel strap that buckles on the side."
  - "Over the years, the Peshawari Chappal has evolved, with modern variations incorporating new materials and designs while staying true to its roots. Today, it is worn by people all over the world who appreciate its style and craftsmanship."
