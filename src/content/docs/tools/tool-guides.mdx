Peshawari Chappal offers a diverse range of handcrafted leather sandals, each with its own unique character and purpose. Below, you'll find guides to help you understand our different styles and choose the perfect pair for your needs.

### Style Guides

**Classic Peshawari Chappal**  
   - Traditional design with crossed straps and open-toe construction
   - Perfect for everyday wear with both traditional and casual outfits

**Zalmi Style Chappal**  
   - Modern interpretation favored by younger generations
   - Contemporary aesthetics with various color options available

**Imran Khan Style Chappal**  
   - Elegant formal design named after Pakistan's cricket legend
   - Ideal for special occasions and traditional ceremonies

**Charsadda Style Chappal**  
   - Robust construction with wider straps for enhanced durability
   - Named after the historic town famous for its leather craftsmen
